floe | version 1.2.2

Probability-ranked word study tool

Current features:
• Words: 3-9 letters, CSW24
• PREV/NEXT/RANDOM navigation
• Jump by rank or word
• Anagram clusters
• Front/back hook indicators
• Front/back truncation indicators
• Auto-scroll mode
• Autosaves your place per word length
• TTS reader (speak)
